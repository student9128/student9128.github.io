下面仅贴出几行关键代码，有兴趣的可以下载完整demo查看详情

View toastView1 = View.inflate(MainActivity.this, R.layout.toast_view1, null);//填充Toast样式
Toast customizeToast1 = new Toast(getApplicationContext());
customizeToast1.setGravity(Gravity.CENTER, 0, 0);//设置吐司位置Gravity
customizeToast1.setView(toastView1);
customizeToast1.show();
